Konyo Museum

An intimate museum and contemporary art gallery established in 2012 to collect, preserve, display, promote and encourage the collection of contemporary Ghanaian art. It organizes changing exhibitions of works in its permanent collection. Konyo is located on a seven acre verdant property that has a stream running through it, 30 minutes from the historic slave castles in Cape Coast and Elmina. Free Range chickens, guinea fowls, ducks, turkeys and a cow, Liberty, run around it. Konyo offers a retreat guest house away from the maddening crown and noise of the cities. Konyo runs the annual Aview: Accra Visual Arts Week during the month of April in various venues scattered in the city of the national capital, Accra.
